Broccoli Beef

As a person that frequents the gym, I love to eat this dish to get my protein for the day at a level that I want. I love broccoli and I love beef, so why not mix the both? This dish requires at least one hour of your time to make and a smile on your face as you smell the sweet aroma of cow meat and and hearty broccoli being cooked together in an air fryer.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Beef
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cornstrach
  • Scallions
  • Low-Sodium Beef Broth
  • Vegetable Oil
